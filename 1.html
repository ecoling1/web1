프로듀스 X 101, 당신의 소년에게 투표하라!

Produce X 101 (Korean: 프로듀스 X 101) is an ongoing 2019 Korean boy group survival reality show on Mnet. This is the 4th season of the TV franchise. The public (called 'national producers') "produces" a boy band by choosing 11 members among the 101 trainees from 47 different entertainment companies and independent trainees through online voting and live voting with multiple elimination rounds. The public will also choose the group's concept, debut song and group name. The show premiered on May 3, 2019.
